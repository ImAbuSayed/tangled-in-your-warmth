[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Caught up in the shadows where the firelight sways
I could lose myself inside your endless gaze
The midnight air is pulling me to your side
There’s nowhere else that I would rather hide

[Verse 1]
A single lantern on the window sill
The world outside is beautifully still
We left the noise an ocean away from here
Just breathing in the quiet when you pull me near

[Pre-Chorus]
A touch that lingers right against the skin
A quiet fire slowly burning in
I’m falling deeper than I ever planned
Just tell me that you’ll take me by the hand

[Chorus]
I’m tangled in your warmth tonight
Caught under the glow of an amber light
Every single breath is an open door
Leaving me waiting and wanting more

I'm tangled in your warmth, I'm yours
